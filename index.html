Award Context

National Recognition

The NIH NOURISH (Nutrition for OUR Immune System Health) Autoimmunity Challenge was a national crowdsourcing competition led by the NIH Office of Autoimmune Disease Research. Autoimmune diseases affect between 23.5 and 50 million Americans, yet the role of diet and nutrition in these conditions remains critically underexplored. The challenge drew submissions from researchers, clinicians, patient advocacy groups, and interdisciplinary teams across the country. Fifteen prizes were awarded in total, and I was honored to be among them.

"The winners showed thoughtful planning and designs that, with further development, could result in innovative solutions to benefit Americans affected by autoimmune diseases."
— NIH Official Press Release, March 30, 2026
